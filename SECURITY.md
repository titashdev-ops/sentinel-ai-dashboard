## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of the Sentinel AI dashboard seriously. If you have discovered a potential security vulnerability, please follow the steps below to report it responsibly.

How to Report
Please submit your findings by emailing [titashdev@gmail.com]. To help us investigate the issue as quickly as possible, please include:

A clear, concise description of the vulnerability.

Steps to reproduce the issue.

Any supporting logs, screenshots, or code snippets.

What to Expect

Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours.

Investigation: We will investigate the issue and provide a status update within 5 business days.

Resolution: If the vulnerability is confirmed, we will work to provide a fix as quickly as possible. We appreciate your patience and your commitment to keeping our ecosystem secure.
